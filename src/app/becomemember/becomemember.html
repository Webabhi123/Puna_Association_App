Why PEEA Membership

  • PEEA identifies and brings together likeminded to represent the voice of Traders, Manufacturers, Contractors etc.

  • We promote active representation in the Government as well as institutions and industry/trade organisations in India and abroad.

  • PEEA represents to the government, problems faced by its members, individually or as a group, to find solutions and provide relief from unreasonable acts of administrative and policy making departments.

  • PEEA provides a bouquet of professional and value-added services to its members.

  • ELCINA services are tailored to serve the best interest of its Members & community to empower it to face competition and prosper. These services are constantly upgraded to suit the changing demands of the environment and needs of the industry.

  • PEEA's E-commerce facilities and its dynamic website together attract about many visitors per day and provide a platform for members.

  • PEEA is a repository of information and research, readily available for its members and helps them in taking critical decisions based on relevant facts.

  • The Executive Committee may choose to admit as a member an organization which does not meet the above criteria, if it feels the same would support the cause and add value for its members.

Who can become a member

  • A Company or a firm engaged or interested in electrical trade shall be eligible to became a Member of Association, provided they have ONE YEAR standing in the business. The Applicant should provided the proof of GST Registration Number & provided the application is approved by the Managing Committee.

  • MEMBERS - can became a Life or Ordinary Member of the Association by paying in full the following Life Member Fees / Annual Subscription Fees / Admission Fees, as application accompanied by the prescribed application form.

  • LIFE MEMBERS

    A Company or a Firm can became a life Members by paying fees, only once. The Life Member Shall have the right of voting similar to Annual Members.

  • ANNUAL MEMBERS

    A Company or a firm can became an Annual Member by paying in advanced, an Annual Subscription Fee together with one time Admission Fee.

    • Application form of oustation Member as prescribed by the Association shall fulfill the conditions as per clause (C) & (D) above.
    • Applicant must be registered member of its local assocation & copy of its membership certificate along with the recommendation letter from its representive assocation should be accompany the application form.
    • Outstation member will not have any Voting Right nor be allowed to be Member of the Managing Committee.
  • Committee Discretion:

    The Managing Committee shall have full discretion and power to decide whether possesses the requisite qualifications to become a member under clause (A) and (E) above. The Managing Committee shall not be bound to assign any reason whatsoever for rejecting any application for Membership and its decision shall be final

SUBSCRIPTION FEES

  • LIFE MEMBERS: Rs. 10,000/- only once (Plus GST as applicable)
  • ANNUAL MEMBERS: Rs. 2,500/- Plus Admission Fee Rs. 1,000/- only once (Plus GST as applicable)

The Annual Subscription shall fall due in the Month of April every year.

IMP. NOTE: No Admission or Annual subscription fee shall be refundable on termination or suspension of Membership.
