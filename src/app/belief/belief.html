Beliefs

Safety is Non-Negotiable
We believe that safety is the foundation of all electrical work and must be prioritized in every job, training, and regulation.

Skilled Trades Are Vital to Society
We believe that electrical professionals are essential to the modern world and deserve respect, recognition, and support.

Education Powers Excellence
We believe in lifelong learning and providing access to high-quality education and training to ensure a competent and future-ready workforce.

Innovation Drives Progress
We believe that embracing technology and sustainable practices is key to advancing the electrical industry and meeting tomorrow’s energy needs.

Integrity Builds Trust
We believe in ethical conduct, transparency, and professionalism in all aspects of our industry and operations.

We believe in the power of partnerships—between members, government, educators, and industry leaders—to grow and protect the trade.

Advocacy Creates Opportunity
We believe that a strong, unified voice can influence policies that benefit electrical professionals, businesses, and communities.

Diversity and Inclusion Empower Growth
We believe in fostering a trade that welcomes and supports people of all backgrounds, creating a stronger and more innovative workforce.
