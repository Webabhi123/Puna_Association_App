Founders Message

To be the leading voice and catalyst for innovation, safety, and excellence in the electrical industry — empowering professionals, advancing standards, and driving sustainable energy solutions for a brighter, connected future.

About Poona Electric & Electronics Association

PEEA is the Pune level body of Traders in electrical, industrial electronics, Licensed electrical contractors and associates like electrical manufacturers, engineers, consultants and similar service providers in India and was founded in . PEEA has become a pioneer association of the Electrical sector having more than 900 active members.

PEEA members represent a combined annual turnover in excess of USD 70 billion. India’s exports of electrical equipment are around USD 12 billion.
