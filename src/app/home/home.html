About Poona Electric & Electronics Association

PEEA is the Pune level body of Traders in electrical, industrial electronics, Licensed electrical contractors and associates like electrical manufacturers, engineers, consultants and similar service providers in India and was founded in . PEEA has become a pioneer association of the Electrical sector having more than 900 active members. PEEA members represent a combined annual turnover in excess of USD 70 billion. India’s exports of electrical equipment are around USD 12 billion. Core Beliefs of an Electrical Trade Association:
Vision
To be the leading voice and catalyst for innovation, safety, and excellence in the electrical industry — empowering professionals, advancing standards, and driving sustainable energy solutions for a brighter, connected future."
Mission
To support and represent electrical professionals by promoting industry excellence, advancing safety standards, providing high-quality education and training, fostering innovation, and advocating for policies that strengthen the electrical trade and its workforce." To provide business development platforms for enabling collaborations and B2B engagement for business expansion and new opportunities.
