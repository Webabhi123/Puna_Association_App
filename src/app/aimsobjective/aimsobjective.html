AIMS & OBJECTIVES - A Commitment of Excellence

Aims:

  • To unite the electrical fraternity and form themselves into a well knit organisation, whose aim is to adopt standards of high business morality and skilled workmanship in pursuance of their activities.

  • To Represent and Support Members & Advocate for the interests of electrical professionals, businesses, and apprentices through policy, partnerships, and resources.

  • To Promote co-operation among persons, firms, companies connected with the electric trade and industry in India, whether as importers, exporters, wholesale or retail dealers, manufacturers, contractors, consultants with a view to their adopting a common policy and collectively taking such steps as may be considered necessary or expedient to further and safeguard the interest of trade and industry.

Objectives:


  • Provide Accredited Training and Certification Programs: Offer relevant, up-to-date education to ensure members meet regulatory and professional standards.

  • Promote Electrical Safety and Compliance: Disseminate safety guidelines, code updates, and best practices to reduce risk and ensure public trust.

  • Advocate for Favorable Legislation and Regulation: Engage with government and regulatory bodies to influence policy in ways that benefit the industry and its professionals.

  • Facilitate Networking and Collaboration: Host events, forums, and working groups to encourage knowledge-sharing and professional connections.

  • Support Business Growth and Development: Provide members with tools, templates, market insights, and group benefits to enhance competitiveness.

  • Increase Public Awareness of the Trade: Promote the value of licensed electrical work and encourage careers in the trade through campaigns and outreach.

  • Champion Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion: Encourage an inclusive industry environment that welcomes talent from all backgrounds.

  • Maintain a Strong Digital and Community Presence: Use modern communication tools to engage members, share resources, and strengthen the association’s impact.
