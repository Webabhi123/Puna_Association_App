History

Association in Brief It was a way back in 1950 thereof in Pune, there were very few Radio selling shops. No exclusive electrical goods selling shops were there. Radio selling shops were having agencies of British make valves radio sets like Ferranti, Marconi, PYE, Bush, etc.

About 15 to 16 shopkeepers started to assemble together solving their day-to-day business problems. Having meetings, picnics off and then in the year 1959 the idea of forming the association was in forehead. “Radio Merchant Association” was formed.

During the period, there was a need of shops selling electrical goods and one by one electric shop started establishing around Budhwar Peth, forming their own Association.

Managing Committee of both Associations accepted the amalgamation of both the Associations. This POONA ELECTRIC & ELECTRONICS ASSOCIATION was formed.

Having heritage of more than 50 years the Association became powerful and started working in a big way with activities like A.G.M., Diary Printing, Gifts, Calendar, Cultural Programmes, Sports, etc. started through different committees.

The Association enlarged activities to keep healthy relations with Govt. Offices, legal body authorities, PMC, etc. It arbitrated with:
1) The Federation of Trade Association New Delhi
2) Maratha Chamber & Commerce

(A) Financial Aspect:
The main fact was that the entrance fees were the only source of income to the Association. Radical activity of increase of income side and tried to raise the income from taking advertisements in the yearly multicolour diary. This activity bore fruit and the Association was put on sound financial footing.

(B) Life Membership Fees Started
Legal Entity:
With huge banks and fixed deposit, having a small rented office at Balaji Mandir, the Association had no legal entity as it was not registered with any Govt. Authority.
The need of registration after the association in getting registered with the Charity Commissioner, after forming a trust and with our own rules & registrations (Constitution).

(C) Change Name: Year _____ New Name – Poona Electric & Electronics Association, Pune. Was taken.

(D) Membership
Efforts of all President & working committee members. The Membership number increased from 150 to 650 members. This enabled the association in raising a hedge fund.

(E) Need of a Bigger Owned Office
In the year 2010 the association became one of the powerful associations in Pune, having membership of All India Electric Dealers Association N.Delhi.
With our activities & performance we could convince Manikchand Dhariwal to help us buy our own office. This new office space on ownership basis was taken at ‘Bedekar Sadan’ Budhwar Peth, Near Electrical Market.

(F) Scope of Activities
These new well-furnished office The Association increased activities in many aspects:

  • Blood Donation Camps, Health Camps
  • Legal Seminars on various law related subjects
  • Picnic & AGM
  • Bulletin & Circulars
  • Sport Activities
  • SMS facility, Arbitration Committee
  • P.U.C. Camps
  • Entertainment for Members
  • Agitations & Protest for injustice in VAT, Octroi, Sales Tax Act, etc.

A new Federation & Trade Association Pune was formed & our Association took an active part in joining these agitations.

The only aim of the association was to benefit and profit the members.
